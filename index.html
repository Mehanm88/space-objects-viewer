Flew closer to Earth than many satellites, coming within 400 kilometers. Its small size meant it would have disintegrated in the atmosphere.

Potentially Hazardous Asteroids

1950 DA

~1.3 km in diameter with a potential risk of impact in year 2880. One of the highest known impact probabilities, though extremely low.

2022 EB5

Impacted Earth's atmosphere on March 11, 2022, over the Norwegian Sea. Detected just hours before impact — the 5th asteroid discovered prior to hitting Earth.

~1.1 km in diameter. Passed safely by Earth on Jan 18, 2022, at ~1.9 million km. No immediate threat.

Gravity

In space, gravity is the main force that pulls objects towards each other. It keeps planets moving around stars, moons circling planets, and helps shape galaxies. Everything with mass has gravity, but bigger objects like stars and planets have a stronger pull. Gravity also bends light, creating effects like gravitational lensing.
